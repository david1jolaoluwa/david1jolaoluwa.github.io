Our Story

Built on Care,
Driven by Results

Aiming High Tutors began with a simple but deeply personal commitment: to support children with patience, care, and teaching that builds confidence as well as academic success. In the early days, this meant travelling from home to home to deliver one-to-one tuition—often with my baby daughter strapped to my back.

Today, parents trust Aiming High Tutors for high standards, personalised support, and a nurturing learning environment where strong foundations are built and every child is encouraged to achieve their full potential.

Our lead tutor is a qualified Science teacher and former AQA GCSE Science examiner with an MSc in Educational Leadership and Management — meaning we understand not just how to teach, but how exams are marked and how individual children learn best.
